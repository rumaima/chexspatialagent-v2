# CXR SpatialAgent — Sample Clinical Questions
# Organised by spatial taxonomy (Section 3.3 of the paper)

# ── Presence / Detection ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Is pleural effusion present in the chest X-ray?
Is pneumothorax present in the chest X-ray?
Is consolidation present in the chest X-ray?
Is cardiomegaly present in the chest X-ray?
Is pulmonary edema present in the chest X-ray?
Is an endotracheal tube present in the chest X-ray?
Is a nasogastric tube present in the chest X-ray?

# ── Spatial Location ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Where is the consolidation located?
Where is the mass located?
Where is the nodule located?
Where is the atelectasis located?

# ── Laterality ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Is the pleural effusion more extensive in the right lung or in the left lung?
Is the consolidation more extensive in the right lung or in the left lung?
What is the laterality of the pleural effusion: unilateral or bilateral?
What is the laterality of the pneumothorax: unilateral or bilateral?

# ── Distribution & Extent ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
What is the distribution pattern of the consolidation: localized, focal, diffuse, unilateral, bilateral, central, peripheral, or multilobular?
What is the distribution pattern of the pulmonary edema: localized, focal, diffuse, unilateral, bilateral, central, peripheral, or multilobular?
What is the extent and location of the mass: localized, focal, diffuse, central, or peripheral?
What is the extent and laterality of the cardiomegaly: localized, focal, diffuse, unilateral, or bilateral?

# ── Containment ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Is the pleural effusion contained entirely within the lung field without extending beyond the pleural surface?
Is the mass contained entirely within the lung field without extending beyond the pleural surface?
Is the consolidation contained entirely within the lung field without extending beyond the pleural surface?

# ── Zone Presence ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Is the consolidation located predominantly in the right lower zone of the lung?
Is the atelectasis located predominantly in the upper zone of the lung?
Is the consolidation located predominantly in the left lower zone of the lung?
Is the endotracheal tube present in the upper zone of the lung?

# ── Relative Positioning ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
What is the relative position of the consolidation with respect to the hilum?
What is the relative position of the mass with respect to the carina?
What is the relative position of the pleural effusion with respect to the diaphragm?
What is the relative position of the carina with respect to the aortic arch?

# ── Device Localisation ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Where is the tip of the endotracheal tube relative to the carina?
Where is the tip of the nasogastric tube relative to the diaphragm?
Where is the tip of the central venous catheter relative to the carina?
Where is the tip of the endotracheal tube relative to the right main bronchus?

# ── Border & Silhouette ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Does the consolidation involve or obscure the left heart border?
Does the consolidation involve or obscure the left hemidiaphragm?
Does the pleural effusion involve or obscure the right hemidiaphragm?
Does the opacity involve or obscure the right heart border?

# ── Appearance ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
What is the appearance of the aortic knob?
